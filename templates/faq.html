Frequently Asked Questions

Is this therapy?

No. Micro-Scenarios is a practice and learning tool. It is not a replacement for therapy, coaching, or legal advice.

How should I use the scenarios?

Most people get the best results by doing one or two scenarios per day, reflecting briefly on why certain choices feel better than others.

Can I use this with my team or clients?

Yes, you can walk through scenarios in workshops, coaching sessions, or training, as long as you respect the licensing terms you choose later.
